About

I'm a painter, producer, and developer living in Austin, Texas.

My background is in traditional drawing, painting, and ceramics, though I've always been curious about and had a knack for technology; I grew up making websites about Legos and video games, and began my studies in traditional software engineering and web development while earning my Bachelor of Fine Arts at UT Austin.

In 2011, I began producing electronic music under the guise of PBS. Skeptic was released on Detroit's Crisis Urbana in 2013 and I have self-released music on bandcamp.

I am looking for new projects, collaborations, and opportunities in software and web development -- please send me a message if you want to connect!

Work

My primary vocation is traditional abstract painting, however in the past few years I've shifted gears toward more direclty working with and understading machines.

This began when launching the PBS project in 2011, and has since directed back to my childhood dream of being a programmer, due in large part to my work expierence developing eBooks and now working for a local technology startup.

My skills and interests lean on front-end development (JS, HTML, and CSS), but I have expeirence and knowledge of back-end technologies as well (Python, SQL). I hope to bridge the gap between the creative and technological in my future projects.
