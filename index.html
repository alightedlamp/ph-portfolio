About

I'm a painter, producer, and web developer living in Austin, Texas. Previously I was working out of Big Medium's wonderful Bolm space and currently I'm making paintings at Pump Project. I make music as PBS and have released records on Crisis Urbana and Always Human Tapes; I've built apps and websites, and am open for contributing to open source development projects.

Work

My primary vocation is traditional painting; however, as time has passed and the state of the world has changed in such a way that has rendered people more vulnerable to those with power in technology, I've shifted gears toward working with and understading machines.

This began with making electronic music on my computer and has since shifted to returning to my childhood dream of being a programmer. One day I suspect these pursuits will all collide into something else, and it's that curiosity I'm primarily oriented towards.
