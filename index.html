About

I'm a painter, producer, and web developer living in Austin, Texas.

I began my painting career working out of Big Medium's wonderful and full-of-character Bolm Studios where I made the majority of the Pour Paintings and participated in Austin's East Austin Studio Tour. The resultant paintings where shown with Big Medium, Las Cruxes, and Farewell Books. Currently I am working on the Domestic Paintings series out of Pump Project.

I also make electronic music under the guise of PBS, released Skeptic in 2013 for Crisis Urbana, and have a forthcoming record, Ruckus, for Always Human Tapes

My most recent work has been centered around the digital, working on building mini-apps and websites, and contributing to open source projects.

Work

My primary vocation is traditional abstract painting, however in the past few years I've shifted gears toward working with and understading machines.

This began through launching the PBS project in 2011, and has since directed back to my childhood dream of being a programmer; now most of my energy and time is spent around working with a building things using modern technology.

One day I suspect these pursuits will all collide into something else, and it's that curiosity I'm primarily oriented towards.
